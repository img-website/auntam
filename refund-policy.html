Booking Cancellation Rights

Driver or Agent can cancel a booking anytime before the driver is available at the pick-up location and agent or driver have clicked – “Pickup Ride”. Post clicking upon pickup ride no booking cancellation can be done.

Refund Policy in case of Cancellation –

Booking Cancellation by agent –

If a booking is canceled by agents before pickup by the driver or arrival point said by the driver will be instantly refunded back to the driver.

Booking Cancellation by Driver –

In case booking is canceled by driver before pickup then an amount of not more than 50% of the total amount paid by the driver is deducted as cancellation charges. And an amount of 50% to 80% is divided between agent and driver depending upon the circumstances of booking cancellation.
